HotelZippo

The design system for a concierge, not a chatbot.

The visual language behind HotelZippo — an AI that replaces 30–40 hours of fragmented family-trip research with one confident, reasoned recommendation. Warm and editorial like a trusted friend; precise and trustworthy enough for a high-confidence researcher.

Airbnb warmth · Linear precision Expressed as Tailwind tokens Web-first · responsive to 375px v1 · Review 1 of 4
Foundations

What the design must protect

Nine UX principles govern the product. Four of them shape every pixel here.

Concierge, not search

No results pages, no filters, no ranked lists. A verdict with reasoning, delivered in conversation.

Hard flags never dilute

Structural issues are always amber or red, always above the fold, never collapsed. The Karon failure must be impossible.

A trusted friend's voice

Warm, specific, personal. Claude's verdicts are set in an editorial serif italic — advice, not a database row.

Weighted honesty

Conflicting reviews shown with percentages, not smoothed into false consensus. Transparency builds trust.

01 · Color

Terracotta, warm stone, and two colors that mean danger

A single warm brand hue carries trust and action. Neutrals are warm-toned, never clinical grey. Amber and red are reserved exclusively for hard flags — semantics, not decoration.

Brand · Terracotta

Defined in OKLCH for perceptual evenness — all steps share hue family ~41°, chroma tapering at the extremes. 500 is the core brand and primary CTA; 600 hover, 700 pressed; 50 tints icon wells and assistant surfaces.

03 · Spacing

A 4-pixel base, all the way up

Every gap, pad, and margin is a multiple of 4. Generous by default — editorial layouts breathe.

04 · Radius

Soft, never bubbly

Inputs and buttons share a 10px radius; cards round to 16px; panels to 20px; badges go fully round. Restraint keeps the product feeling editorial rather than playful.
